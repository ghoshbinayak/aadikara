R E B E C A

Reunion and Bengal Engineering College Annuals

From it's very inception in 1932, this has been the annula reunion of the then BE College. Later on it has been transformed into an annual college fest cum reunion, well known for its magnanimity in eastern India. On this 78th edition of REBECA, we believe the unity amongst the students and the ever growing presence of our alumni will be more evident than ever before.

Classical Night

The first day of our auspicious fest decorated with amazing performances by well known classical singers and dance groups.

BEings Night

Breath taking performances by our students portraying their talent, including a dance competition 'oscillations'.

Kolkata Night

Spell bounding performances by renowned artists and bands from kolkata, giving local artists a huge platform to show their talent.

Mumbai Night

Sensational performances by various stars from our very beloved Bollywood.
